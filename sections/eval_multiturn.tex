\subsubsection{Multi-turn conversation evaluation}

We use two evaluation frameworks for multi-turn conversations:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{PatientSim-style}: PatientSim \cite{patientsim} evaluates differential diagnosis accuracy, persona fidelity, profile consistency, and doctor quality. We adapted the doctor quality evaluation and added patient realism and lab evaluation.
    \item \textbf{HealthBench-style}: HealthBench \cite{healthbench} is a benchmark from OpenAI with 262 physicians from 60 countries, 5,000 conversations, and 48,562 unique rubric criteria across seven themes. We extracted universal rubrics that apply across conversations, excluding prompt-specific criteria tied to individual scenarios.
\end{enumerate}
